Frequently Asked Questions

You can report issues directly through our mobile app by selecting the bike or station and choosing "Report Problem." Alternatively, call our 24/7 emergency line (+44 (0)117 32 81999) for immediate assistance with safety issues.

If a station is full, use our app to locate the nearest available station. If you're unable to return the bike due to technical issues, contact customer support immediately at +44 (0)117 32 81000 to avoid additional charges.

We offer corporate memberships, station sponsorships, and promotional partnerships. Email partners@bikzo.co.uk with details about your organization and partnership interests.

Bikzo is Bristol's premier bike-sharing service, committed to sustainable urban mobility.
