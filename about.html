Who We Are

Bristol's premier bike-sharing service committed to sustainable urban mobility.

Founded in 2024 by UWE Bristol students, Bikzo began as a class project to solve urban mobility challenges. Today we operate 1,200+ bikes across 50+ stations serving thousands of riders daily.

Our mission is to make sustainable transportation accessible while reducing traffic congestion and carbon emissions in Bristol.

Our Journey

2024 - Humble Beginnings

Launched with 50 bikes at UWE Frenchay Campus as a student project

2025 - City Expansion

Expanded to 20 stations across central Bristol with 300 bikes

2026 - Electric Fleet

Introduced electric-assist bikes to help with Bristol's hills
